Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Vadrifts.

Why Vadrifts?

Vadrifts is one team's home for everything we put out — Roblox scripts, web apps, userscripts, extensions. The bar stays the same across every project: clean, free, fast, and actively maintained. We rose from the ashes of Vastral and Valorium with the same legendary bypassing power you remember, but now there's a whole lot more going on alongside it.

Is it actually user-friendly?

Yes. Every project is built for people who'd rather use the thing than read a wiki. ArrayField, our scripts, the image converter — pick it up, get the result, close the tab. No fake onboarding flows, no twelve-step setup, no premium walls hiding the actual feature you came for.

Is it safe?

Yes. Our Roblox scripts are obfuscated, undetected, and updated constantly — the chat bypasser has been clean for over 8 months. The web apps and tools never log your data and the source is right there on GitHub if you want to audit it yourself. Your account safety is our top priority.

Where do I get a key (for the Roblox scripts)?

Most of our Roblox scripts use a simple Discord key system. Join discord.gg/WDbJ5wE2cR and follow the instructions in the key channel. Keys are free and take less than 30 seconds. Some scripts (like our Horrific Housing one) are completely keyless. Our non-Roblox projects don't use keys at all — just open and use.

Do these work on mobile?

Yes. The site is fully responsive, our Roblox scripts work with mobile executors (Delta, Codex, Krnl mobile, etc.), and the web apps are tested down to small phone screens. Some of the heavier tools obviously prefer a real keyboard, but nothing is gated behind desktop-only.

How do I suggest a project or report a bug?

Use the suggestion box right above — it pings us in Discord directly. For anything code-related you can also open a GitHub issue. We read everything; we just can't always reply the same hour.

Still have questions? Hop in our Discord — we're around.
